Linguine Bolognese

Description:

Enjoy this classic Italian ragu. Full of flavour from good quality tomatoes, garlic, red wine and an assortment of herbs for a real flavour hit!

Ingredients list:

  • Linguine pasta
  • 450g Ground beef
  • Fresh Roma Tomatos
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Celery
  • 100ml Red wine (optional)
  • 3 Tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • Small bunch of Fresh Rosemary, Thyme and Basil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large sauce pan over medium-low heat.
  2. Add the onion, celery, carrot, bay leaf and garlic. Cook gently for 10 minutes until soft.
  3. Add the minced beef and cook for another 5 minutes until browned.
  4. Add the chopped herbs. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Turn the heat up to med-high, pour the wine and simmer until nearly evaporated.
  6. Add the tomatoes and season with salt & pepper. Reduce heat to low and simmer gently for 30-45 minutes until sauce is nicely thickend and rich.
  7. Meanwhile, cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
  8. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce. Toss well to coat.
  9. Enjoy!

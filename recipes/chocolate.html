Chocolate Brownies

Description:

Indulge in these rich, fudgy chocolate brownies. Perfect for satisfying your sweet cravings!

Ingredients list:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Remove from heat and stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until smooth.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts (if using) until evenly distributed.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it out evenly.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs, but not wet batter.
  7. Allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares.
  8. Enjoy your homemade chocolate brownies!

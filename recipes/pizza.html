Gracey's Papa Jim Pizza

Description

Learn how to make a Gracey’s signature charred and crispy “Papa Jim” pizza.

Ingredients:

  • 1 x 260g dough ball
  • Sprinkle of semolina
  • 1 tbsp garlic olive oil
  • 1 tbsp grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • 1 tbsp grated fresh Fior Di Latte
  • 2 tbsp torn aged Fior Di Latte
  • 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 6 pieces spianata salami
  • 10 black olives

Optional finishes: Sprinkle of basil and Parmigiano-Reggiano

Steps:

  1. Preheat your Gozney oven to 800°F / 420°C (stone floor temperature).

  2. Shape your dough by using your fingers, gentle push the air to edge of the pizza to make a nice crust, flip the dough and continue. The pizza should be around 13 inches. Use semolina if your dough is sticking.

  3. Drizzle the garlic olive oil over the base and sprinkle over the Grana Padano, the grated Fior Di Latte and the aged Fior Di Latte.

  4. Spoon over the tomato sauce and make 6 small mounds of sauce, sprinkle the dried oregano onto the pizza and tear over the spianata salami. Place the black olives on top.

  5. Place onto the pizza peel, dust off the semolina and turn off the oven. Allow the base to cook and then, using the placement peel make the first turn – it is easier to use the placement peel to start and then use the turning peel. Turn the oven on again and continue to cook for 3-4 minutes until the pizza is nicely coloured.

  6. Remove the pizza from the oven and slice, finish with a sprinkle of basil and parmigiano-reggiano if you like!
